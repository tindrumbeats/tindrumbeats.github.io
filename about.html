ABOUT TINDRUM

A film/video production company, where films are made with love, passion and dedication.

In Gunter Grass novel The Tin Drum, to "beat a tin drum" when used as an idiom means to create a disturbance in order to bring attention to a cause. This is based on an interpretation of the book where Oskar's beating "his tin drum symbolizes his protest against the middle-class mentality of his family and neighborhood."

Through Tin Drum, we are here to create ripples in your minds and souls. We want to break into your comfort zone and shake you up. We want to make you cringe, sit up and think.

We need your attention to those aspects of this world which have for too long been brushed under the carpets of decency, reverence, comfort, glamour, commercial interests and sanctimonious bliss. Hence, with your support, we’ll continue to make films that matter and challenge your cerebrum. In the future, we’ll want you to make films for us. We’ll want to provide platforms to produce your stories. In that direction, Tin Drum is taking the first step here.
